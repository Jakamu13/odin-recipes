Soup

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious bowl of chicken noodle soup. It will ensure a full and warm belly.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth
  • Rotini Noodles
  • Chopped Carrots
  • Chopped Celery
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. In medium saucepan bring 3 cup of chicken broth with rotini noodles to a boil
  2. Once broth is starting to boil add chopped carrots and celery
  3. While vegetablcs are in the broth, chop chicken breast into cubes pieces, add to the saucepan
  4. Let cook covered over medium heat for 10 minutes
  5. Uncover and let the chicken noodle soup cool for 3 minutes, then enjoy!
