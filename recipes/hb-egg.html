Hard Boiled Egg

Description

This is a recipe for a nice firm hard boiled egg

ingredients

  • egg

Steps

  1. Put desired amount of eggs in a pot on high and bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling for 2 minutes turn off heat and put lid on pot
  3. Let sit in pot covered for 15 minutes
  4. After time, uncover pour out hot water and peel shell off egg
